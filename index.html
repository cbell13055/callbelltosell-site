Meet Jessica Bell

Local guidance with a polished, personal approach.

Jessica Bell is a dedicated real estate professional serving Clayton, Johnston County, and the greater Raleigh area. Known for her thoughtful guidance and clear communication, she helps clients navigate buying and selling with confidence from start to finish. Originally from South Jersey, Jessica grew up in Turnersville before relocating to Clayton in 2022. She quickly fell in love with the community and now proudly calls it home with her husband and their two boys.

Real estate is more than a career for Jessica—it’s a core part of her life. With a background rooted in real estate investment and a family actively involved in both residential and commercial ownership and development, she brings a deeper understanding of the market beyond the typical transaction. Whether working with sellers preparing to maximize their home’s value or buyers searching for the right fit, Jessica takes a steady, strategy-first approach focused on results, relationships, and long-term success.

Whether you are selling your home, buying your next one, relocating, or simply trying to understand your options, Jessica brings clarity to the process from the first conversation to closing.
